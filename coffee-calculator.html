Understanding Your Coffee Costs

The "latte factor" is a term coined by financial author David Bach, referring to the small, regular purchases we make without thinking that add up to significant amounts over time. A daily $5 coffee might seem insignificant, but over a year, it can cost you over $1,800.

This calculator helps you visualize the true cost of your coffee or drink habit. Use these insights to make informed decisions about your spending. Even small changes, like making coffee at home a few days a week, can lead to substantial savings over time.
