Why Track Your Daily Habits?

Most people underestimate how much their small, daily habits cost them over a lifetime. A $5 coffee every morning seems harmless, but over 10 years with 7% compound interest, that money could grow to over $25,000. A pack-a-day smoking habit can cost over $3,500 per year — and over $50,000 in a decade when you factor in health costs.

HabitCalculator helps you see the true cost of everyday habits. Our free, easy-to-use calculators cover coffee and drink spending, smoking and vaping costs, subscription services like Netflix and Spotify, dining out and takeout expenses, and daily savings and investment growth with compound interest.

Whether you want to quit smoking, cut back on takeout, cancel unused subscriptions, or start saving just a few dollars a day, our calculators give you the motivation and clarity to make better financial decisions. All calculators support 15+ currencies with real-time exchange rates.

Popular Calculators

Coffee Cost Calculator — How much does your daily Starbucks habit really cost? Enter your drink price and frequency to see weekly, monthly, yearly, and 5-year projections.

Daily Savings Calculator — See how $1, $5, or $10 per day grows with compound interest over 1 to 30 years. The power of consistency is remarkable.
